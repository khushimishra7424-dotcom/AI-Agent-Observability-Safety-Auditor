EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY

All full-time employees are entitled to 18 paid leave days per calendar year, split into
12 casual/sick leaves and 6 earned leaves. Leave requests must be submitted through the
HR portal at least 3 working days in advance, except for medical emergencies.

Unused earned leaves can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of 15 days.
Casual and sick leaves do not carry forward and lapse at the end of the calendar year.

Maternity leave is granted for 26 weeks as per company policy, and paternity leave is
granted for 2 weeks. Both must be applied for at least 30 days before the expected date,
except in cases of premature delivery.

Employees on probation (first 3 months) are eligible for a maximum of 4 leave days,
and any additional leave will be treated as loss of pay unless approved by the reporting
manager in exceptional circumstances.

Public holidays are declared separately by HR at the start of each year and are not
counted against the annual leave balance.
