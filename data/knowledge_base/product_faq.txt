PRODUCT FAQ - CLOUDSYNC BACKUP SERVICE

CloudSync offers three plans: Basic (10GB, free), Pro (500GB, ₹299/month), and
Business (2TB, ₹999/month). All plans include automatic daily backups and 256-bit
AES encryption for data at rest and in transit.

Refunds are provided within 14 days of purchase if the customer has used less than
5GB of storage. Refund requests must be raised through the support portal, and
processing takes 5-7 business days.

The Pro and Business plans include file versioning for the last 30 days, allowing
users to restore any previous version of a file. Basic plan users get only the
latest version and no version history.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all Business accounts and optional
but recommended for Basic and Pro accounts. Accounts inactive for more than 12 months
on the free Basic plan may have their data archived and eventually deleted after
a 90-day warning period.

Customer support is available via email for all plans, and live chat support is
available only for Pro and Business plan subscribers, Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 9 PM IST.
