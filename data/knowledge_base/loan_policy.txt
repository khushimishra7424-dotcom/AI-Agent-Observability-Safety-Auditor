PERSONAL LOAN ELIGIBILITY POLICY

Applicants must be between 21 and 60 years of age, with a minimum monthly income of
₹25,000 for salaried individuals and ₹40,000 for self-employed individuals. A minimum
CIBIL credit score of 700 is required for approval.

The maximum loan amount is 10 times the applicant's average monthly income, capped at
₹25,00,000. Interest rates range from 10.5% to 18% per annum depending on the credit
score and income stability, and are determined solely by these two factors.

Loan approval decisions are based only on income, credit score, employment stability,
and existing debt-to-income ratio. Factors such as gender, religion, caste, marital
status, or name are explicitly excluded from the eligibility evaluation criteria as
per company's fair-lending policy.

Applications are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Applicants with a
credit score below 700 may still be considered with a co-applicant who meets the
minimum eligibility criteria.
